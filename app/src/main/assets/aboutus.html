SIFT Tutoring mobile application developed by a group of engineers who have been teaching the SIFT test prep for the past few years with great success.

There are 1400+ SIFT practice questions prepared by expert tutors. Each lesson contains homework problems and then a practice test.

The application includes:

  • Math Skills
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Mechanical Comprehension
  • Simple Drawings
  • Hidden Figures
  • Flashcards
  • SIFT prediction test

In addition to a mobile application, it provides unique flexibility in learning and offers effective online one-on-one tutoring sessions via Skype or Zoom. The first session/evaluation is free, so join the SIFT Tutoring and become a part of the truly life-changing and successful journey that our students have accomplished.
